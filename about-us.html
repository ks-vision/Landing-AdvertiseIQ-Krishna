OUR STORY

Why We Built AdvertiseIQ

We were Amazon sellers before we were a software company. We felt the pain firsthand.

2019

The Problem

Our founding team was managing Amazon ads manually — spending 30+ hours a week on bid adjustments, keyword research, and reporting. There had to be a better way.

2020

The Prototype

We built the first internal tool to automate bid adjustments based on ACOS targets. Our own accounts saw a 28% ACOS reduction in the first month.

2021

First Customers

We opened AdvertiseIQ to 20 beta brands. Within 90 days, all 20 had renewed and brought referrals. We knew we had something.

2023

AI Platform Launch

We launched the full machine learning bid optimizer and keyword intelligence suite — the product you see today.

Now

500+ Brands & Growing

AdvertiseIQ manages over $2.8B in Amazon ad spend for brands across 15+ marketplaces. The mission continues.
